Description

Location: NIE5-B2-04
Number of Badminton Courts: 9
Number of Basketball Courts: 3
Facilities Offered: PE Storeroom 1


The Indoor Sports Hall is located in Blk 5 of National Institute of Education (NIE)

and it is highly used by Physical Education teacher trainees for multiple purposes.

The Indoor Sports Hall is air-conditioned and one can control the light switches from the switchbox.

However, it is important that you book the Courts from the PE Facilities Office before utilising the courts.

This ensures that you do not disrupt any ongoing/upcoming classes that are conducted in the Indoor Sports Hall.


A C T I V I T I E S


These are some activities that you can try out in the Indoor Sports Hall!

There are many different equipment in the PE Storeroom,

such as basketball balls, badminton rackets and shuttecocks, frisbees,

skipping ropes, volleyball balls and net!

There are many other equipment that the store holds, and fret not if the equipment you desire is not in the store!

You can always visit the PE Facilities Office for more equipment!
