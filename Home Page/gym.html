G Y M N A S I U M

Description

Location: NIE5-B1-08
Number of Badminton Courts: 2
Facilities Offered: Mini Storeroom


The Gymnasium has two different floorings. As you enter the Gymnasium,

you are greeted by a rubber flooring, which allows you to set up two badminton courts.

As you walk deeper into the Gymnasium, you would notice that there is a change in flooring,

the wooden flooring is ideal to hold Gymnastic lessons and to practice your Gymastics skills.

It allows easy set up for Gymnastic lessons as the cushions and mats lie perfectly on the wooden flooring.



A C T I V I T I E S


Think that class photos can be mundane?

You can always visit the Gymnasium to lay down the mats and take such class photos!

If these class poses are a bit too advanced for you, you can start off learning Gymnastics by trying out these poses with a partner!

It is never too late to learn Gymnastics and the abundance of Gymanastics equpiment in the Gymnasium would always be there for you to try out!
