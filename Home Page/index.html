SPORTS BLOCK IN NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF EDUCATION


Welcome to the Sports Block in National Institute of Education (NIE)!!

This block is a vibrant block that consists of many outdoor venues and it is highly used by Physical Education and Sports Science student teachers.

As a Physical Education(PE) student teacher, I would be more than happy to give you a tour around the Sports Block in NIE.

During one's free time, one can spend some time outdoors playing basketball, hockey and many other sports while studying in NIE.

As you are playing sports in these outdoor venues, you can use the opportunity to make new friends!


To gather more information on the facilities that the sports block offers, feel free to visit the Physical Education and Sports Science website!
